Our Services

We offer you

Personal Care and Assistance

- Help with daily living activities such as bathing, dressing, grooming, and mobility.

- Support with medication management and health monitoring.

Community Participation and Social Engagement

- Assistance with accessing community activities, social events, and recreational opportunities.

- Support with building social skills and fostering relationships within the community.

Supported Independent Living (SIL)

- 24/7 support for individuals in shared or individual accommodation to live more independently.

- Assistance with household tasks, meal preparation, and daily routines.

In-Home Support

- Personalized support in the home environment for tasks like cooking, cleaning, and laundry.

- Light maintenance and organization assistance to improve quality of life at home.

Skill Building and Capacity Development

- Training in life skills, including budgeting, cooking, personal care, and household management.

- Support with literacy, numeracy, and digital skills to promote greater independence.

Transportation and Travel Assistance

- Safe, reliable transport services to appointments, community activities, and daily errands.

- Assistance with travel training for those looking to gain independence with public transport.
